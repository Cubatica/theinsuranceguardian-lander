
# The Insurance Guardian.com Project Plan & Requirements

## Project Overview
**Project Name:** The Insurance Guardian.com Auto Insurance Site  
**Owner / Lead:** Christian Encinas  
**Project Goal:** Build a professional, trustworthy auto-insurance website to satisfy Google compliance, capture leads, and establish credibility.

---

## Objectives
1. Create a **main homepage** that looks professional and trustworthy.  
2. Publish **supporting content**:
   - Terms & Conditions page  
   - Privacy Policy page  
   - Optional blog posts or articles related to auto insurance  
3. Ensure the site passes **Google trust metrics** (SSL, functional links, credible content).  
4. Implement **lead capture form** for collecting potential customer information.

## Homepage Requirements
**Sections to include:**  
1. **Header:** Logo, navigation menu (Home, About, Services, Blog, Contact).  
2. **Hero Section:** Headline, subheadline, and prominent lead capture form.  
   - Example headline: "Reliable Auto Insurance in Charlotte"  
   - Example CTA: "Get a Free Quote Today"  
   - Form fields: Name, Email, Phone, ZIP  
3. **Services Section:** 3–4 key insurance services or benefits.  
   - Example: Full Coverage, Liability Coverage, Roadside Assistance, Free Quotes  
4. **About Section:** Brief credibility paragraph about the company.  
5. **CTA Section:** Optional repeat of lead capture form.  
6. **Footer:** Contact info, social links, links to Terms & Privacy pages.  

**Design Notes:**  
- Clean, minimal layout with whitespace.  
- Use subtle auto-themed graphics.  
- Mobile responsive.  
- Optional JS for form validation or alerts.  

---

## Legal Pages Requirements
**Terms & Conditions (`pages/terms.html`)**
- Services offered  
- Limitations of liability  
- User responsibilities  
- Contact info  
- Example snippet:  
```html
<h2>Terms & Conditions</h2>
<p>By using The Insurance Guardian.com, you agree to the following terms...</p>
```

**Privacy Policy (`pages/privacy.html`)**
- Collection of user information from forms  
- Use of collected information  
- Data protection measures  
- Example snippet:  
```html
<h2>Privacy Policy</h2>
<p>The Insurance Guardian.com collects your information solely to provide quotes and services...</p>
```

## Workflow / Steps

1. **Project Setup:** Create folder structure and open in Cursor.com.  
2. **Homepage Development:** Header, hero, services, about, CTA, footer. Add CSS/JS.  
3. **Content Creation:** T&C, Privacy Policy, optional blog posts.  
4. **Lead Capture Form:** Include Name, Email, Phone, ZIP.  
5. **Testing & Review:** Check links, responsiveness, SSL, form submissions.  
6. **Deployment:** Upload to theinsuranceguardian.com hosting.  
7. **Final Verification:** Confirm Google trust metrics, functionality, and credibility.
